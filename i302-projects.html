I302

Academic Success in the Digital University
August - December 2022

As part of the class I302, Academic Success in the Digital University, I developed a portfolio website to showcase my project from this class and what I learned from it.

I learned how to conduct research through the UT Library, put that research into writing, and present my research through a video presentation and a website.

Research Project

My project was about researching the effects of COVID-19 on wealth inequality in the US. I learned how to research on the UT Library website, construct a research proposal from my findings, present that information through a video with captions, and use html, javascript, and CSS to create a website to explain these findings.

Group Project

For my group project, my group and I collaborated to address the issue of how technology and work have been impacted by COVID-19. This project taught me how to work better with my peers, and how to put together massive amounts of collective information and research into a digestible project.

Reflection Project

My reflection project was created at the very end of the semester to reflect on the coursework from this class and what I learned from it. This assignment allowed me to consider the skills I have gained from taking I302, and what I will be able to use them for in the future, such as basic HTML coding, knowledge of how to email professors, and how to gather academic resources.
